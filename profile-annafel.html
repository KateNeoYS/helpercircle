Notes from her employer

Drawn from Trang’s recommendation letter.

Annafel has spent the past year with Trang and Nigel’s family, who recommend her warmly as they prepare to relocate to the UK.

They trust her completely with their young daughter — her daily routines, her extracurricular activities, and her learning. She supports the child’s Kumon, encourages her with her homework, and accompanies her through her Chinese home tuition.

Trang describes someone honest and reliable, trusted enough to do the family’s grocery shopping with their credit card. She is cheerful, kind and patient with children, follows instructions well, and carries out her work diligently.

Her real passion is cooking. She prepares a wide range of cuisines — Western, Chinese, Thai, Vietnamese and Indian — follows recipes with ease, and has completed several cooking courses. Many of her dishes are favourites with the family’s children and friends.

What sets her apart, Trang says, is her positive attitude: polite, respectful, always willing to listen and learn, and gracious with feedback.
