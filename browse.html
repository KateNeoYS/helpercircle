Referred helpers in Singapore

A small, growing community of helpers introduced to us by previous employers. We're onboarding new referrals as families share them with us.

Our approach

How a referral becomes a profile

We don't list helpers from a database. Each profile starts with a family reaching out about someone they've worked with personally — and ends with us making an introduction.

01

A family reaches out

An employer in Singapore tells us about a helper they've worked with, why she's looking for a new home, and what she's good at.

02

We verify and listen

We speak with both the referrer and the helper. We confirm her work history, her preferences, and what she's hoping for next.

03

Her profile goes live

Once we're confident in the referral and the helper is ready, we publish her profile here for families to meet.

Be notified when new profiles go live

We'll send you a short email when we introduce a new helper. No newsletters, no marketing — just a quiet note when there's someone new to meet.
