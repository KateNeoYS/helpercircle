“When it was time for our helper to move on, we wanted her to join a good family where she could continue providing her service and experience.

What stood out to me was the transparency throughout the process. Questions were openly discussed, expectations were clarified, and there were no hidden costs or hidden agendas.

The Zoom meeting organised by Helper Circle was especially useful because the helper, current employer and prospective employer could all meet together, discuss concerns openly, and make sure everyone was comfortable before committing.

I would happily recommend Helper Circle to families passing on a helper they trust, and to families hoping to welcome a referred helper into their home.”

— Sheila
