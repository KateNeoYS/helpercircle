Notes from her employer

Drawn from Tanielle’s reference letter, with earlier letters from past families on file.

Tanielle has shared a warm reference for Katrine, who has been an integral part of her family’s home, caring for their three young children, a newborn, a toddler and a preschooler.

She describes Katrine as mature, professional and warm, with an extraordinary ability to stay calm, organised and dependable in a busy family home.

Her strengths with children stand out. Tanielle writes that she is naturally nurturing, patient and attentive, and highly capable of managing several children at once, confidently supporting meals, bathing, sleep schedules, school preparation, developmental play and outings.

Beyond childcare, she manages the household to a high standard, cooking, laundry, grocery shopping and upkeep, working independently and with real pride in a clean, well-run home.

What Tanielle highlights most is her integrity, reliability and discretion: respectful, easy to communicate with, quick to learn, and consistently willing to go above and beyond.

Katrine holds an education degree and was a teacher in the Philippines before coming to Singapore, which shows in her thoughtful, developmental way with children. Earlier families describe the same, a helper who cared for their little ones from babyhood and whom the children still remember fondly.
