About Siti Nur

Siti Nur is an Indonesian helper from Bandung who has lived and worked in Singapore for six years. She spent her first two years with a young couple in Tampines, then four years with a family in Bishan — the same family who referred her when they recently moved overseas. She is calm, self-organised, and good at managing her time without needing much direction. She speaks conversational English and basic Mandarin.

From her previous employer

"Siti has been with us for four years and she's been wonderful. Honestly, we were sad to let her go. She's reliable, the kids love her, and she never needed reminding about anything. We'd take her back in a heartbeat."

Why she was referred

Michelle's family relocated to London for her husband's work. They didn't want Siti to have to return to Indonesia, so they referred her through Helper Circle. The referral was submitted in September 2024, and we verified the contact details and employment history before listing her.
