A few honest words from you — whether you referred a helper, hired one, or joined a new family — help us keep Helper Circle thoughtful and trustworthy for the families and helpers who come next.

Why we ask for feedback

  • It helps us improve the experience.
  • It helps future families make informed decisions.
  • It ensures testimonials on Helper Circle are genuine and permission-based.

Who reads this feedback?

Every submission is read by a real person. Feedback helps us improve the experience and, with permission, may be featured on the website to help future families learn from others' experiences.
