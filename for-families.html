For clarity

What this isn't

  • —
    Not an agency. We don't source or recruit helpers.
  • —
    No commission on her salary. She keeps her full pay.
  • —
    No bulk biodata. Profiles are added one at a time.
  • —
    No pressure on you. Walk away if it doesn't feel right.

And on a practical note

  • +
    No return flight to buy. A local transfer means you skip the air ticket home — typically S$300–600.
  • +
    A small token of thanks. A modest gesture when your referral is successfully placed — passed forward, donated, or for you.
