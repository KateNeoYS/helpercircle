Kartika S.

Indonesian · Elderly care & household

Referral verified — vouched for by her current employer, Sheila

Kartika has been working with Sheila's family in Singapore, caring for Sheila's 85-year-old mother. Sheila is now helping Kartika find a good next family by the end of June, when Sheila's long-time previous helper returns to her household.

Before her current role, Kartika completed a two-year contract with a Singapore family whose school-age children were twelve and fifteen.

Sheila describes her as warm, easy to talk to, and quietly very good at her work. She's sincere and straightforward — she follows a given schedule well, doesn't need reminders, and asks when she doesn't understand something rather than guessing her way through. She's turning forty, fit and active. She's the kind of helper who has the day's routine running — the laundry done, the ironing finished, the kitchen spotless — sometimes before Sheila is awake.

Her steady, calm temperament shows up in everything she does. She has a real affinity with people too: in her current home she's built a warm rapport with Sheila's Cantonese-speaking mother and gets on with her easily.

She's most at home in a close, kind household — a family that looks after one another. She's a confident kitchen assistant rather than an independent cook for elaborate cuisines, and her petite build means she's best matched to elderly care where the person isn't large.
