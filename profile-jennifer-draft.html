Notes from her employer

Written from Vinnie's referral — she has shared a detailed picture of how Jennifer works.

Jennifer — Jenny to those who know her — is Filipino, Catholic, and 48 years old. She has been working in Singapore since 2009, with college-level education behind her. She has been with Vinnie's family for around two years, joining a few months before their first daughter was born in January 2024, and has been part of their family through the newborn and toddler years with both their toddler and their four-month-old baby.

Vinnie describes her as warm, emotionally mature, patient, and observant with children — calm but firm, able to set boundaries without raising her voice. She engages children well through dancing, singing, drawing, and play. Vinnie trusts her completely to take their daughter to playgrounds and school pick-ups.

Beyond childcare, Jennifer is an independent all-rounder who can run the household, market, cook, clean, and manage routines. She has high standards, strong attention to detail, and rarely forgets things. She's proactive with good judgement, particularly around children and household priorities. She cooks Western, Chinese, Filipino, and some French dishes, and enjoys baking.

Best fit. In Vinnie's words: "Jenny would be ideal for a family looking for one strong, independent, all-rounder helper who can manage children, household chores, cooking, marketing, and the overall home routine. Given her experience, she works best with employers who are comfortable giving her autonomy and trusting her to manage proactively."

Area to note. Vinnie is honest about this: "Jenny is experienced and can be quite set in her ways. She is open to listening to feedback, but may naturally default to her own way of doing things. She would not be suitable for families who prefer to closely micromanage helpers."

The reason for the move: Vinnie's family is shifting to a two-helper setup with two very young children and a busy household. Jennifer prefers to be the only helper in the household, and prefers all Sundays off rather than alternating rest days with another helper. As Vinnie puts it: "This is a difficult decision for us, as Jenny has cared for our children with real love and responsibility."
