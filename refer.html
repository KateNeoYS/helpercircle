What happens next

How your referral becomes a profile

Every referral goes through the same careful process — whether the helper is your former employee or someone you're vouching for.

1

Confirmation email — within minutes

You'll receive an email confirming we've received your referral, along with a short summary of what you submitted.

2

We reach out to the helper — within 2 business days

We message her on WhatsApp to introduce ourselves, confirm she'd like to be referred, and gather a few more details (work history, photo, availability).

3

Verification call with you

If you've shared your contact, we'll call or email you with 2–3 quick questions about her — her strengths, work style, and why you're vouching for her. About 5 minutes.

→ This step earns her the "Referral verified" badge on her profile.

4

We write and share her profile draft

We turn your input plus our conversations into a calm, editorial profile. Both you and the helper see it before it goes live, with a chance to suggest edits.

5

Profile goes live — you'll be the first to know

Once published, we email you with the link. If she finds a new family through Helper Circle, we'll send your thank-you token (your choice of voucher, donation, or pass-it-forward).
