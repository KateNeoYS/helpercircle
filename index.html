Why Helper Circle exists

Good helpers shouldn’t be hard to find.

When a good helper needs a new family, word spreads through Facebook groups and WhatsApp chats, then disappears within days. Helper Circle gives trusted helpers one place to be found, so the right families see them in time.

“anyone know a good helper?” forwarded, then lost gone in days
→ One trusted circle
How trust is built

Two signals, shown on every profile.

However a helper reaches us, her profile only goes live once it carries one of these two signals, and we’ve reviewed it.

🏆 Employer Recommended

A family she worked for vouches for her.

A current or former employer recommends her directly, and we confirm it with them. Our strongest signal.

📅 Long-Term Family Retention

She stayed with one family for years.

Several years with a single family, checked against official MOM records. Her record speaks for itself.
